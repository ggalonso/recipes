## Macaroni Instructions

1. Boil water to bubble
    * Add salt to speed up the boiling process
2. Once boiled, add macaroni
3. Boil for 7 to 8 minutes
4. Test the macaroni at the 6-minute mark to ensure al dente
5. Drain water, and put macaroni in large bowl

## Assembly Instructions

1. Before Assembly (If necessary)
    * Slice smoked ham into small squares
    * Cut carrots into small cubes
    * Slice black olives into small pieces
2. Pour all ingredients (except Paprika) over macaroni
3. Mix thoroughly, add additional ingredients such as Mayonnaise/Mustard if necessary to suit taste
4. Add Paprika on top for flavor
