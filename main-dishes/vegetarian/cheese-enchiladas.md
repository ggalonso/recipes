# **Cheese Enchiladas**

* Prep Time: 10 - 20 minutes
* Cook Time: 10 - 20 minutes
* Serving: 7 - 9
* Temperature: Broil

## Overall Ingredients

<center>

| Ingredient                        | Measurment |
| --------------------------------- | ---------- |
| Neutral Oil (Vegetable, Avocado)  | 1 cup      |
| Corn Tortillas                    | 20-25      |
| Medium Cheddar, And Oaxaca Cheese | N/A        |

</center>

## Enchilada Sauce Ingredients

<center>

| Ingredient                            | Measurment      |
| ------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Unsalted Butter                       | 1 tbsp          |
| Neutral Oil (Vegetable, Avocado)      | 1 tbsp          |
| All Purpose Flour                     | 3 tbsp          |
| Guajillo, and New Mexico Chili Powder | 4 tbsp (2 each) |
| Onion Powder                          | 2 tsp           |
| Garlic Powder                         | 2 tsp           |
| Ground Cumin                          | 2 tsp           |
| Oregano Powder                        | 1 & 1/2 tsp     |
| Salt                                  | 1 & 1/2 tsp     |
| Low Sodium Chicken Broth              | 3 cups          |
| Tomato Paste                          | 2 tbsp          |

</center>

## Tortilla Instructions

1. Pour 1 cup neutral oil into pan in medium heat
2. Add tortilla for a few seconds or until it bubblets
3. Repeat process with rest of tortillas

## Enchilada Sauce Ingredients

1. Medium heat
2. Add butter and neutral oil
3. Once melted add all purpose flour, cook for 1 minute
4. Add chili powder, and spices
5. Mix with wisk for ten seconds
6. Add 1 cup of chicken broth, wisk while pouring
7. Add rest of broth, continue to wisk while pouring
8. Add tomato paste, wisk until disolve and comes to a simmer

## Assembly Instructions

1. Add half of sauce into baking sheet
2. Fill tortilla with cheese
3. Roll tortillas and place in baking sheet
4. Cover enchiladas with rest of sauce
5. Add more cheese on top
6. Broil for a few minutes

> **Note**<br>
> To add more spice, add 1 tbsp of Cayenne Pepper
